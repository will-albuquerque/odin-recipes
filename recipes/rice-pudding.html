Rice Pudding

Description

Creamy and cheap, the perfect easy desert.

Ingredients

  • 100g pudding rice
  • 25g caster sugar
  • 700ml whole milk
  • butter, for the dish
  • ground nutmeg, to top

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 140°C
  2. Grease a shallow oven dish with butter
  3. Add the pudding rice and caster sugar to the dish
  4. Cover with the milk
  5. Sprinkle the ground nutmeg over the top
  6. Cook for 90min, ensuring the rice is tender
